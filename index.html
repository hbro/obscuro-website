Obscuro

Obscuro is the incantation of a Transfiguration spell used to conjure a blindfold over the eyes of the victim,
therefore obstructing their view of their surroundings.

The domain obscuro.be is owned by Hans Broeckx and is mainly used to
hide his main e-mail address from intentional or unintentional malicious actors.
